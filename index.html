About

Chaincode Labs exists to support Bitcoin and related technology. We have been running Bitcoin educational programs since 2016 ([2016], [2017], [2018 Bitcoin], [2018 Lightning], [2019]). All of our programs are free of charge.

Online Seminars

Weekly video calls diving deep into security concerns, adversarial thinking, and approaches to Bitcoin and Lightning protocol design.

Residencies

In person programs designed to accelerate the learning of open-source contributors.

  • spend more time designing protocols based on Bitcoin and/or Lightning
  • think about edge cases and security models
